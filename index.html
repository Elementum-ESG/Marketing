The Financial Win-Win

A Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) allows you to price your solar energy competitively. It creates a gap where the tenant pays less than the grid rate, but you still make a healthy margin.

  • Grid Rate: ~30p/kWh (High)
  • Your PPA Rate: ~20p/kWh (Competitive)
  • Solar Cost: ~5p/kWh (Low LCOE)
Result: Tenant saves 10p/kWh. Landlord profits 15p/kWh.
